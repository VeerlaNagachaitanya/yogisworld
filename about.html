About Yogi's World

For years children and their families have enjoyed attending storytimes, checking out materials and participating in a variety of other programs.
Yogi's World came about to provide children with the virtual experience of going to the Library and participating in the same types of activities the Library offers at its physical locations. First launched in 2020, Yogi's World has been newly redesigned to present favorite stories and activities in a format compatible with desktop and mobile devices. The site also now includes early literacy information for parents and caregivers.
